Short Bio

I am currently a Research Fellow at GAP Lab with Prof. Xiaoguang Han. Before this, I received my B.S. from Nanjing University of Science and Technology in June 2014 under the tutelage of Prof. Ligang Liu, and my Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology of China in June 2021 under the joint supervision of Prof. Ligang Liu and Prof. Xiaoguang Han. During my Ph.D., I visited City University of Hong Kong in 2017, advised by Prof. Hongbo Fu. From March 2018 to March 2023, I worked at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen as a visiting researcher advised by Prof. Xiaoguang Han.
